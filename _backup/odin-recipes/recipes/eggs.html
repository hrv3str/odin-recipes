Scotch Eggs

Description

These Scotch eggs make for a delicious and easy special occasion recipe for your family. We serve ours on Christmas Eve over a bed of lettuce with sliced tomatoes and mustard for garnish.

Ingedients:

  • Oil for frying
  • 4 large eggs
  • Bulk pork sausage
  • Flour
  • 4 large eggs beaten
  • Dried blead crumbs

Steps:

  1. Hit an oven and fryer
  2. Boil eggs and peal
  3. Flatten sausages and make a patty around each egg, flour and roll in bread crumbs
  4. Deep-fry to the golden-brown. Bake for 10 min
  5. Cut in half and serve
